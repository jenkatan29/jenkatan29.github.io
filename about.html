About Jen

Jen works with interactive mediums lie AR, VR, installation, and food. She often engages with tangible manifestations of complex data structures through playful, sensory interactions, and highlights the tightly wound link between individual and global systems. She believes the audience and artist work in tandem and on the same power/playing ground, and her goal is to create worlds where that relationship can exist. Jen wrote this jenself.
