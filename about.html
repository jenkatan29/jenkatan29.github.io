About

Jennifer Katanyoutanant works with interactive mediums like VR, installation, and food to create objects and experiences that facilitate communication and co-creates systems of sharing and exchange. Her projects have explored ecological manifestations of the Youtube recommendation algorithm, creative communities around emerging technology, and cultivating remote intimacy through food and live web performance.
