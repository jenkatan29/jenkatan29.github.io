Creative Producer

What exactly do I do?

Do I work on the business side or come up with ideas? Do I develop projects independently or join teams of specialists? Why 'creative' producer instead of just producer?

The answer is..............yes

I build media, experiences, and events for people to connect, play, and learn; creating structures that help teams move towards a common vision. Typically this means collaborating with artists, designers, and engineers, creating production plans, translating between strategy, finance, and production, and presenting to clients.

I've worked with cultural organizations, digital agencies, game studios, universities, NGOs, Fortune 500s, and more. Want to work with me? Reach out
